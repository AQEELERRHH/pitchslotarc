How it works
01

Creator registers

Set your minimum bid threshold. Your attention has a price. The market decides the rest.

02

Company bids

Submit a message + USDC bid. Funds lock in Arc escrow instantly. No payment without a reply.

03

AI agent filters

Your agent ranks bids by amount and quality score. Spam never reaches you. Only the best do.

04

Reply = payment

Accept a bid and USDC releases to your wallet instantly. Reject it and the bidder gets refunded in full.

05

14-day auto-refund

No response after 14 days? USDC automatically returns to the bidder. Zero trust required.
